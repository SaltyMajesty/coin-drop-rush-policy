Privacy Policy

Coin Drop Rush

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Summary: Coin Drop Rush is a simple arcade game. We collect minimal data and respect your privacy. We use third-party advertising services which may collect data as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Payment information

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Game Data: High scores stored locally on your device only
  • Device Information: Basic device info for app compatibility (device type, OS version)
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners may collect device identifiers for ad personalization (see Section 3)

2. How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used to:

  • Save your game progress and high scores locally
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Third-Party Advertising

We use Meta Audience Network to display advertisements in our app. Meta may collect and use data including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

For more information about Meta's data practices, please visit:

  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network Privacy

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads:

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

4. Data Storage & Security

Your game data (high scores) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data and it is not transmitted to any servers.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data processed through our app.

5. Children's Privacy

Coin Drop Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play.

Advertisements shown are filtered for general audiences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Reset your game data by clearing app data or reinstalling
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data processing
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
